Girls' Generation

ABOUT

It's been 17 years since Girls' Generation entered the K-pop scene. The eight-member girl group from SM Entertainment has set countless records and is considered a legend act. And rightly so: Countless idols who subsequently debuted were inspired to make music by Girls' Generation! The girl group is therefore a piece of K-pop history!

SNSD is the abbreviation of the group's Korean name "Sonyeo Shidae", which is literally translated to "Generation of Girls". Sonyeo Shidae can also be shortened to "SoShi". New fans are often confused by the girl group's many names, but they ultimately all stand for the same thing.
